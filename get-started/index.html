Unlimited trading pairs

Gunbot does not limit the number of trading pairs, like USD-BTC or BTC-LTC, you can simultaneously trade.

It's up to you to trade just one or a few pairs, trade hundreds of pairs or even run 100 different bot instances with each a single pair.

Flexibility: API slot system

Thanks to the API slot system you always control on which exchange you use your bot. Switching to another exchange or API key is easy and free.

An exchange slot enables you to register an exchange API key to your Gunbot license.

Feature information

Gunthy: Gunbot utility token

Gunbot uses a blockchain based license system. It uses a utility token called Gunthy on the Ethereum blockchain.

Each license comes with a free number of tokens, which need to be held in the wallet address you register during your purchase. In case you sell the tokens, your bot would stop running until the token balance is as expected again.

Feature information

Enhance your strategy with confirming indicators

Using confirming indicators makes Gunbot strategies even more flexible. It allows you to narrow down the exact market situation where you want Gunbot to trade.

Besides indicators built in in strategies, like MACD or Bollinger Bands, you can use a range of confirming indicators like RSI, Stochastic, ADX & more.

Feature information

Trail prices to maximize your results

Trailing allows your bot to hold back on a trade as long as price keeps moving in the same direction within a specified range.

This technique greatly improves your entry- and exit points. While your indicators might say it's time to buy, trailing will allow your bot to wait until prices stopped dropping.

Feature information

Margin trading

Use Gunbot for margin trading on supported exchange. Currently only Bitmex is supported for margin trading with native Gunbot strategies, expect more exchange support soon.

Feature information

Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA)

DCA is a highly effective method to profitably trade pairs when prices start dropping.

It works by aggressively reducing the average price per unit by buying more units at lower prices until a profitable sell is in sight.

Feature information

Reversal Trading (RT)

RT is a Gunbot technique to accumulate more coins when prices keep dropping, without investing more than paid for the initial buy order.

It works by first selling your assets, then buy more units back at a lower price. Trading fees are accounted for.

Feature information

AutoConfig

With AutoConfig you can automate config changes for Gunbot.

Options range from automatically adding trading pairs to dynamically adjusting trading strategies based on conditions you define.

  • Scan exchanges to automatically add pairs based on your own filter criteria
  • Scan exchanges to remove pairs from your config
  • Change the strategy for active pairs
  • Complete automation for pair specific strategy parameters

Feature information

Backtesting add-on

Evaluate your strategy before you run it. Backtest almost any Gunbot strategy option on tradingview.com.

As long as your trading pair is available on TradingView, you can backtest your strategy.

Feature information

TradingView add-on

Use Gunbot to execute trading alerts sent from a custom strategy at TradingView.

It works by listening to incoming emails from TradingView, when an alert comes in Gunbot will immediately execute it.

You can also use Gunbot in a mixed mode where it listens to incoming alerts and processes a native strategy simultaneously. This way you can, for example, buy using an alert and sell using Gunbot trailing.

Frequently asked questions

Not sure about something? Wonder what the difference between packages are?

Here are the answers to some of the most common questions we hear from our users.

I'm new to bot trading, will you help?
Sure! Don't be afraid to ask questions, we'll help you out.

We know this software can be a bit intimidating to start with. That's exactly why we started this Gunbot support community.
Which strategies are available in Gunbot Starter?
You can use the Emotionless, Gain and BBTA strategy in Gunbot Starter.

They can be freely combined too, for example you could have the bot buy with BBTA and sell with emotionless.
Is Gunbot difficult to learn?
Gunbot is easy to start with, and admittedly hard to fully master - as with any complex software project.

Be prepared to learn something new every week, it's fun though!
What is GUNTHY token?
Gunbot uses a system to validate license ownership through the blockchain. To do this, it uses a utility token called "GUNTHY" that runs on the Ethereum blockchain.

The goal of this system is to not depend on a central license server. An added benefit is that you can even sell the tokens when you don't need the software anymore.
