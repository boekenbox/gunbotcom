Frequently asked questions

Not sure about something? Wonder what the difference between packages are? Here are the answers to some of the most common questions we hear from our users.

I'm new to bot trading, will you help?
Sure! Don't be afraid to ask questions, we'll help you out. We know this software can be a bit intimidating to start with. That's exactly why we started this Gunbot support community.
Is Gunbot difficult to learn?
Gunbot is easy to start with, and admittedly hard to fully master - as with any complex software project. Be prepared to learn something new every week, it's fun though!
Which strategies are available in Gunbot Starter?
You can use the Emotionless, Gain and BBTA strategy in Gunbot Starter. They can be freely combined too, for example you could have the bot buy with BBTA and sell with emotionless.
What are Gunthy tokens?
Gunbot uses a system to validate license ownership through the blockchain. To do this, it uses a utility token called "GUNTHY" that runs on the Ethereum blockchain. The goal of this system is to not depend on a central license server. An added benefit is that you can even sell the tokens when you don't need the software anymore.
